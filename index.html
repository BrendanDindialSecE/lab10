My Favorite Art Styles

Impressionism

Impressionism is known for its focus on capturing the impression of a moment rather than precise details. Artists in this genre often used loose brushwork and vibrant colors to depict scenes of everyday life, nature, and cityscapes, particularly paying attention to the effects of light and atmosphere.

Landscape Art

Landscape art is a genre that focuses on capturing natural scenery, including mountains, valleys, forests, rivers, and coastlines. Artists in this genre aim to convey the beauty and atmosphere of the outdoors, often depicting various seasons, times of day, and weather conditions to highlight natureâ€™s diverse moods and qualities. It can range from realistic, detailed portrayals to more abstract interpretations that emphasize color, light, and texture.

Surrealism

Surrealism art is a genre that explores the subconscious and dreams, blending reality with fantastical, often bizarre elements to challenge viewers' perceptions. Surrealist works combine unexpected objects, strange landscapes, and distorted figures to create dreamlike scenes that defy logic. This genre encourages freedom of imagination, aiming to reveal deeper truths or emotions by bypassing the constraints of rational thought.
